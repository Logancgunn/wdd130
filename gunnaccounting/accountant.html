Our Founder: Amy Gunn

With more than 20 years of experience our founder has helped numerous people with their accounting needs. It all started when she was a little girl, her father himself was also an accountant. She spent several afternoons with him and learned more about accounting, and decided that it was something that she wanted to do as a career. She has worked for several businesses and has made a name for herself amongst all of those whom have had the pleasure to have her around. She decided that she wanted to help far more people though, and that is when she started her own business.

Amy has several qualifications, she has achieved her bachelors degree in accounting, she has also been certified to train others in quickbooks, she has earned several certifications from schoolings all over Oklahoma where she resides.

Amy is also the mother of 5 Children, ranging from 2 who have joined the United States Marine Corps to her youngest is finishing his time in Elementary School. She has had the joy of watching them grow up into respectable young people in this world, and is glad for this business which helped her see them just a little bit more, and in a different light. She has been married for over 25 years now and has the hopes of being able to travel the world!
